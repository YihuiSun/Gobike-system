Dataset structure¶

There are 5054 bikes in this dataset with 1,863,721 rows and 8 features (duration_sec, start_time, endtime, start station_name, end_station_name, bike_id, user_type, bike_share_for_all_trip.) Most variables are numeric apart from the user_type and location names which are objects.

plt.xlabel('Duration (Minutes)');
plt.title('Average Bike Trip Duration (Minutes)');
plt.ylabel('Number of Trips');

The average bike lasts between 1 and just over 100 minutes with the majority of bike trips lasting just under 10 minutes.
